Day 5: Introduction to AWS and the Management Console

AWS Account Setup:

Create an AWS account using your email address.
Choose a strong password and set up MFA for security.
Be aware of the AWS Free Tier limitations.
AWS Management Console Exploration:

Log in to the AWS Management Console.
Explore the console interface and familiarize yourself with the navigation.
Browse different AWS services categorized by functionality (compute, storage, networking, etc.).
Proof: Take screenshots of different sections of the AWS Management Console.
AWS Regions and Availability Zones:

Understand the concept of AWS Regions (geographical locations) and Availability Zones (isolated locations within a region).
Identify the AWS Region closest to you.
Proof: Take a screenshot of the AWS Management Console showing your selected region.
